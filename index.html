About Schadenfreude

Schadenfreude is home to one of Europe's most prestigious collections of German automobiles, showcasing over a century of engineering excellence, innovation, and design.

Our collection spans from pioneering vintage models to cutting-edge contemporary vehicles, each representing a significant chapter in the story of German automotive history.
